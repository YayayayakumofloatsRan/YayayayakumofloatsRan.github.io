ABOUT

A public notebook for systems, sky, and first-principles work.

Most of the engineering energy here points toward Holo, my primary personal AI runtime. I care about whether memory, tool transport, active threads, online canaries, and runtime state can be inspected and reasoned about as contracts instead of hidden behind a chat skin.
